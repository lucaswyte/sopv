Sex On Premises Venues

This survey is being conducted by a post-graduate student at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) as part of a capstone business project in marketing.

The aims of this survey are to gain a deeper understanding of the reasons why men go to sex on premises venues and their needs being met, identification of the servicescape factors used by sex on premises venues as points of differentiation in market segmentation and to discover the relevance of these factors to the customer experience and their influence on preference of venue.

The survey should take around ten minutes to complete. The information gathered is purely for academic purposes and will be used toward the final report. Your participation is entirely voluntary and your anonymous responses will remain confidential. If you have any questions regarding this survey, please contact the subject coordinator Dr Nigel Bairstow at nigel.bairstow@uts.edu.au.
